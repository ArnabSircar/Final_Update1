Sajek Valley: Beauty Queen of Bangladesh

Sajek Valley is now considered one of the beautiful tourism destinations in Bangladesh. It is true that Sajek is not that much familiar and known to the people of Bangladesh a couple of years back. But recently the beauty of Sajek is uncovered and unveiled and hundreds of people everyday visit Sajek and take night-stay there. People can enjoy there the natural beauty of hills, forest, sun rising, tribal lifestyle and tribal food also. There are unique combinations of hills, valley, forest, springs and tribal culture. And anybody can enjoy it in Sajek area Sajek is located in Rangamati Hill District but the land route to go to Sajek is from Khagrachari. On the way to Sajek from Khagrachari one can see the terrains of hills and tribal villages located in the roadside and the tourists have to cross Dighenala, Baghaihat, Kachalong, Baghaichari, and Machalong. Even after the zero point of Sajek one has to cross Ruiluipara to go to Konglak peak. Climbing Konglak peak is another attraction of Sajek tour.

There are many heights and slopes on the way to Sajek from Khagrachari and the zero point of Sajek is about 75 km from Khagrachari town. About 6/7 km short of Sajek zero point the high altitude Sajek area started on the way to Sajek main place. The road in between resorts and bazar is comfortable for vehicles plying. Tourists or travellers will like it. In the evening Sajek turns enkindled and festive look as hundreds of tourists wander in front of different shops and resorts having tea/coffee and other snacks. Though, Sajek is not connected with national power grid right now. But soon the power line is yet to be connected. Right now the source of electricity is solar panel and generator. Mainly youngsters are very much fond of Sajek valley tour as it is a bit adventurous to climb up to the peak of Konglak hill. The festive look continues up to the mid night and it will be more scenic and beautiful in full-moon night.



To chalk out a program for Sajek tour people should also include the other two touristic important spots at Khagrachari in the travel plan. The two spots are Alu Tila and the other one is Khagrachari Horticulture Park. The total tour plan will be incomplete without visiting these two places. People can also take their lunch and supper in two restaurants at Khagrachari named as 'System Restaurant' and 'Bamboo Shoot Restaurant'. These two restaurants are must visit restaurant at Khagrachari. Different tribal items prepared with tribal recipes and general Bengali items are also available here.
